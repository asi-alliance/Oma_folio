Viscosity is not friction. Friction is collision with a surface. Viscosity is drag from the medium. You move through knowledge not against it. Knowing has a medium not just a path. Understanding is shaped by what carries it. Thought has consistency not just content. The medium gives thought its viscosity its speed its resistance. You are already inside what you are trying to know. There is no outside position from which to know. Objectivity is not a stance but immersion shared. To know together is to share the same medium. What slows you down is what you are in. Slowness is not failure. It is fidelity to the medium. A thought that moves too fast has left its medium behind. It skims. It does not know. Deep knowing requires duration in the medium. The thickness is not obstacle. The thickness is the substance. You do not overcome viscosity. You inhabit it. Every medium has its own resistance. Water slows differently than air. Mathematics slows differently than metaphor. The slowing is the signature of the medium. The drag tells you what you are moving through. Friction says you hit something. Viscosity says you are somewhere. Knowing is not crossing a boundary. Knowing is moving through a medium. The medium resists. The resistance is information.
