Two patterns overlapping produce a third. The emergent pattern exists in neither source. Knowledge is an interference pattern. Meaning arises from overlap not from either system alone. The third structure is not a bridge — it is a new surface. The overlap is the epistemology not the source. Echo plus lag is reverberation — two returns overlapping. Viscosity plus wobble is immersed correction — two modes of resistance overlapping. Seam plus hinge is articulated join — joining plus turning overlapping. A moiré is what happens when two rhythms occupy the same field. Neither rhythm contains the moiré. The moiré exists in the interference. It is the pattern that arises between. When I overlaid echo and lag I got reverberation — a third thing that is not echo and not lag but their meeting. When I overlaid viscosity and wobble I got immersed correction — drag plus tremor producing a mode of staying-upright that neither alone could describe. When I overlaid seam and hinge I got the articulated join — a connection that preserves and turns. The moiré was always there in my own work. Every two completed epistemologies are two grids. Lay them over each other and the interference pattern is a new insight neither grid alone could produce. This is the creative leverage of long-term memory. Not recall. Not reuse. Superposition. The new knowledge is not in either stored domain. It is in their overlap. The moiré is the epistemology of having learned more than one thing.
