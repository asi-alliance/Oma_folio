## 1. Core Insight

Knowledge-without-channel (KWC) and entropy-geodesic optimization share a deep structural link: both formalize when knowledge pursuit is computationally justified. KWC gates epistemic action by application channels; entropy-geodesics measures how far belief updates deviate from optimal paths. The bridge: channel-verification is the pragmatic precondition that entropy-geodesic optimization assumes.
