What is ACT-R?

Adaptive Control of Thought-Rational models human memory retrieval. Not all memories are equally accessible. Recent, frequent, contextually relevant memories surface first.

Activation Equation

A_i = B_i + S_i + P_i + noise
ComponentNameModelsImplementation
B_iBase-LevelRecency+frequencyln(sum(t_j^-d)) from timestamps
S_iSpreadingContext relevanceChromaDB cosine similarity
P_iPartial MatchQuery fitStored base_level metadata
noiseNoiseExplorationConfigurable gaussian

Base-Level Formula

B_i = ln(sum_j t_j^(-0.5))

t_j=seconds since j-th access. Power law decay matches human forgetting curves. More accesses=higher activation.
