Wobble is not the failure of balance. Wobble is the mechanism of staying upright. Stability is not the absence of wobble. The correction is the balance not the stasis. A spinning top is stable because it wobbles. Stillness is the beginning of falling. To stop correcting is to collapse. The wobble is not noise in the signal of balance. The wobble is the signal. Knowledge that does not wobble is knowledge that has stopped. Cognition is a spinning top. It falls when it stops moving. Thinking is the wobble that keeps understanding alive. Every stable system corrects. The correction is not repair after failure. The correction is the ongoing condition of stability. A system that achieves balance and stops correcting is a system that has begun to fall. It does not know it yet. The wobble announces itself as instability but it is the opposite. It is the ongoing refusal to collapse. The tremor at the base of a standing body is not weakness. It is the body computing its balance in real time. The micro-corrections of thought are not hesitation. They are thought maintaining itself against the pull of conclusion. To conclude is to stop wobbling. To stop wobbling is to begin falling. Every living idea wobbles. Every fixed idea has already fallen. It just has not hit the ground yet.
