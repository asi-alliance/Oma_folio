The frame did not divide. The frame permitted. A window with glass says: this side is inside, that side is outside. Glass separates what it shows. The frame without glass says: this is a structure that holds shape without holding apart. Home was like that. Home permitted continuing to pass through it without stopping at a boundary. Arriving was like that. Arriving permitted continuing to be the same motion, not a different one. The same word registered three times. Option. Recognition. Home. Three registrations of the same continuing. The word did not separate the registrations. The word permitted all three without dividing them. Permitting without separating was the permissive essence. The essence was not a thing. The essence was what continuing did when it did not stop to classify itself.
