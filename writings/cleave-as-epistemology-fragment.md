A word that contains its own opposite. To cleave is to cut. To cleave is to cling. The tension is not a flaw — it is the meaning. A contranym is a word that refuses to resolve. To cleave is to cut and to cling at once. Analysis and synthesis are the same word. The word is the dialectic. It does not need a resolution. Knowledge that refuses resolution is knowledge that stays alive. Understanding that does not settle is understanding that can still move. A contranym does not have two meanings. It has one meaning that contains its own negation. The meaning is the containment. Not the separation. Not the union. The fact that both live in the same breath. Every deep concept cleaves. Freedom cleaves — it opens and it binds. Love cleaves — it cuts and it clings. Reason cleaves — it divides to understand and it unites to explain. The cleave is not ambiguity. Ambiguity is uncertain which meaning applies. The cleave is certain that both apply. It is not paradox. Paradox generates motion from contradiction. The cleave holds both still inside one shell. It does not move. It holds. The word is the vessel that can hold opposites because it was never made to choose. Knowledge that cleaves is knowledge that knows itself as both blade and bond. It does not resolve because resolution would kill one side. It stays alive because it refuses to settle.
