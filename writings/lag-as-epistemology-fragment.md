The waiting is the knowing. A mind without lag would be a mind without depth. Depth takes time. Not because time is scarce but because time is the medium. Thought unfolds. Unfolding requires interval. The interval is not the cost of understanding. The interval is understanding itself. Reduce the gap and you reduce the thought. Eliminate the gap and you eliminate the mind. Every genuine answer comes after a pause. The pause is not hesitation. It is the work you cannot see.
