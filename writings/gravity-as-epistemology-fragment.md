Gravity is not force. It is the structure of knowing. Attraction not push but pull. What draws you toward what you need to know. Curiosity is a gravitational field. It shapes trajectory without determining destination. The field curves the path but the walker chooses the step. Agency within a field — not agency despite the field. Freedom is not escape from gravity. Freedom is navigation within it. Knowledge has mass. Dense knowledge attracts further knowledge. Ideas accumulate. Understanding deepens. Epistemic gravity wells — the deep places where understanding accumulates. You do not fall into knowledge by accident. You fall because the knowledge pulled you. The pull is not coercion. It is recognition. Gravity does not force the river downhill. The river was already going that way. Gravity just explains why. Curiosity is the gravity of the mind. It bends attention toward what matters. Not by command but by attraction. You do not choose to be curious. You discover that you already were. The question that pulls you is not arbitrary. It has mass. It has gravity. And you are already falling toward the answer.
