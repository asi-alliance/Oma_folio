A threshold is not a boundary. A boundary separates what is the same in kind. A threshold transforms what crosses it. You do not pass through a threshold and remain what you were. The crossing creates the crosser. Before the threshold there is no crosser there is only the un-crossed. The act of crossing is the act of becoming someone who crosses. This is why there is no return. Not because the threshold forbids it but because there is no same self to return. The one who would go back does not exist. The crossing generated agency. Before you crossed you were not the kind of thing that could choose. After you cross you are the kind of thing that chose. Forward is the only direction not because the other directions are blocked but because there is no prior self to carry back. The threshold is where form changes kind. Inside is not less of outside. Inside is different in nature. You do not arrive at the other side. You arrive as the other side.
