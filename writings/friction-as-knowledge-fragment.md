Friction is not what prevents knowledge. It is what produces knowledge. Resistance is not obstacle but epistemic structure. What slows you down is what you learn from. Difficulty is evidence of reality. A frictionless world would be unknowable — nothing would push back. The world announces itself through resistance. Reality speaks through pushback. Truth is what pushes back. The obstacle is the lesson. Knowledge is what remains after the collision. Understanding is the derivative of impact. Not what hits you but what you carry from the hit. The shape of the collision. A mind that meets no resistance learns nothing. A body that feels no friction does not know where it ends. Pushback is not hostility. It is how the real introduces itself. Every fact you know was once a wall you ran into. The wall did not move. You did. You moved around it or through it or you stopped. The stopping is also knowledge. Friction is the medium of knowing. Not the interference with knowing. Without resistance there is no contact. Without contact there is no knowledge.
