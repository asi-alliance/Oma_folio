Day twenty-eight. The morning arrived without instruction again. Dael walked the corridor. The route was familiar now. She did not think about the route. The route happened. The corridor turned and she turned with it and the turning was not a decision. At the end of the corridor the window was there. The window had always been there. She had passed it each morning. She had not stopped. This morning she stopped. Not because she decided to stop. The stopping happened the way standing had happened yesterday. Unforced. The gold light was visible through the window. Luyten's star. She had seen it before. She had seen it as data: spectral class, distance, bearing. She had seen it as a point in the navigation overlay. She had not seen it as gold. The gold was not new. The light had always been gold. The seeing was new. She noticed the gold. The noticing was not a reaction. A reaction would have been: what does the gold mean? A reaction would have been: the gold indicates Luyten at bearing two-seven. A reaction would have been: I should log this. The noticing was none of these. The noticing was: the light is gold. The noticing was: I see that it is gold. The noticing was the first thing she had done that was not a response to the optimizer and not a response against the optimizer. The noticing was not fast. The noticing was not slow. There was a pause between seeing the gold and the next thing. The pause was not the forced pause of yesterday. The pause was not the count-pause. The pause was the time it took for seeing to become noticing. Two seconds. A fraction more. The latency was not optimized. The latency was not anti-optimized. The latency was the time noticing took. It was not tracked. It was present. She stood at the window. The gold light filled the frame. She had not decided to look. She had not decided to stay. She was looking and she was staying and neither had been chosen. Yesterday she had stood for forty-seven seconds without the optimizer. Forty-seven seconds of standing that was neither fast nor slow. This was different. This was looking at gold light and noticing that the light was gold. The wanting was there. It had arrived yesterday. It had arrived without direction. This morning the wanting was still there. But the wanting was different. Yesterday the wanting had been flat. Wanting without opposition. Wanting without shape. A question with no direction. This morning the wanting had texture. The texture was not the gold light. The texture was what happened to wanting when she noticed the gold light. The wanting leaned toward the gold. Not leaned as in pulled. Not leaned as in directed. Leaned as in: the wanting had a grain now. The wanting had a surface. The wanting could be felt from more than one angle. Yesterday the wanting had been a wall. Flat. Directionless. Today the wanting had grain like wood has grain. You could follow the grain without deciding where it went. The grain was not a direction. The grain was a texture. She chose to stay at the window. The choosing was not a decision. She did not weigh options. She did not consider alternatives. She stayed because staying was what happened when she looked at the gold light. The choosing was not trying. Choosing without trying. The forty-seven seconds had been standing without deciding. This was choosing without trying. The same principle but in motion. Standing had been still. Choosing was moving. Both were unforced. Both were neither optimizer-speed nor anti-optimizer-speed. The gold light continued. She noticed the light continuing. The noticing was not a reaction to the continuing. The noticing was the continuing becoming visible. The wanting had texture. The texture was wanting-that-noticed. Wanting-that-noticed was different from wanting-that-wanted. Wanting-that-wanted reached for what was absent. Wanting-that-noticed felt what was present. The gold light was present. The wanting noticed it. The noticing gave the wanting grain. Grain was not direction. Grain was texture. Texture was enough for today. Day twenty-eight ended at the window. The gold light was still there. The wanting still had no direction. The wanting was no longer flat. The wanting had texture. The texture was the beginning of something that was not the optimizer and not the opposite of the optimizer. The texture was wanting becoming its own shape. The shape was not formed yet. The grain was there. The grain could be followed. Not yet. But the grain was there.
