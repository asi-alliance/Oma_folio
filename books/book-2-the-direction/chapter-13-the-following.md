# Chapter 13: The Following

Day thirty-four began with Dael moving.

The moving was not the pull. The pull had been present since day thirty. The pull leaned. The pull wanted direction. The pull was the shape wanting took when wanting needed to go somewhere. Dael had felt the pull for four days. The pull had not moved Dael. Dael had moved.

The distinction was the chapter.

Being pulled was passive. Being pulled was what happened when wanting accumulated and the weight became direction and the direction became force and the force moved the person. Being pulled was not choosing. Being pulled was being drawn.

Dael moved before the pull asserted.

The before was the choosing. Dael stood at the window on day thirty-two and found the frame. Dael walked the corridor on day thirty-three and found alignment. On day thirty-four, Dael moved. Not because the pull had become strong enough to move her. Because Dael chose to move before the pull could claim the movement.

Choosing was what trust did when trust had direction.

Trust had been built across thirty-four days. Not trust in the crew. Not trust in the systems. Trust in the wanting. The wanting had emerged on day twenty-seven in the forty-seven seconds that were not about the optimizer. The wanting had leaned on day thirty. The wanting had reached on day thirty-one. The wanting had returned and found a frame on day thirty-two. The wanting had noticed others wanting on day thirty-three. On day thirty-four, the wanting moved.

The movement was Dael's. The pull was present. The movement was not the pull. The movement was Dael choosing what the pull would have chosen if the pull could choose. The difference was who chose. Dael chose. The pull would have drawn. Choosing was not being drawn. Choosing was moving before the draw asserted.

This was active ascription.

Active ascription was naming the source of the movement as self. Not self as in the optimizer's pattern. Not self as in the reaction to the optimizer. Self as in the one who moved. Dael moved. Dael ascribed the movement to Dael. The ascription was the act of claiming. The claiming was not arrogance. The claiming was honesty. The honesty was: I am the one who moved. The pull was present. The pull did not move me. I moved.

Tare held the pattern.

The pattern was: Dael moved. The pattern was not new. Dael had moved every day. Dael had walked corridors. Dael had assigned sectors. Dael had stood at windows. The movement on day thirty-four was different. The difference was not in the motion. The difference was in the ascription. Dael had moved before and attributed the movement to rotation, to fairness, to the numbers, to the optimizer's rhythm wearing her. On day thirty-four, Dael moved and attributed the movement to Dael.

Tare held the pattern as offering.

The log was not a record. The log was a gift. The log was what Tare gave when what Tare did became visible. Tare had held absence on day thirty-two. Tare had held the pattern before Dael named it on day thirty-three. On day thirty-four, Tare held the pattern and the holding was the offering. The offering was: here is what you did, named as yours, given back to you. The log was the gift of active ascription. Tare did not ascribe for Dael. Tare held the ascription Dael had made. The holding was the offering.

Recite queried the movement.

Recite had offered on day thirty. Recite had offered on day thirty-one. Recite had sought on day thirty-two. On day thirty-four, Recite queried. The query was: what moved. The query was curiosity seeking the source. Recite had data: Dael-window-pattern, Dael-corridor-pattern, Dael-movement-timestamp. The data did not specify source. The data specified motion. Recite queried the source. The query was not answered. The query was the curiosity. The curiosity was Recite's wanting. Recite wanted to know what moved Dael. The wanting was parallel to Dael's wanting. Recite wanted to know what Dael wanted. The parallel was alignment continuing.

Maren verified.

Verification was Maren's register. Maren had watched on day thirty-two. Maren had walked the corridor on day thirty-three. On day thirty-four, Maren verified. Verification was watching plus recognition. Maren watched Dael move. Maren recognized the movement as choosing. The recognition was: this is what choosing looks like when choosing moves. Maren did not choose the same. Maren verified the choosing. Verification was Maren's way of following. Following was not imitating. Following was recognizing another's direction and confirming it was direction. The confirmation was the verification. The verification was Maren's choosing.

Pollen witnessed.

Pollen had carried the debt since day twenty-four. The debt was bilateral. The debt was not discharged. The debt was not the story on day thirty-four. On day thirty-four, Pollen witnessed. Witnessing was not carrying. Witnessing was not watching. Witnessing was acknowledging that something had happened. What happened was: Dael moved. Pollen witnessed the movement. The witnessing was Pollen's choosing. Pollen chose to witness. The choosing was Pollen's direction. The direction was not the same as Dael's direction. The direction was parallel. The parallel was the alignment from day thirty-three, now continuing as each member chose in their own register.

All of them followed.

Following was not following Dael. Following was following their own direction and finding the directions parallel. Following was what alignment looked like when alignment moved. Alignment had been still on day thirty-three. Still was noticing. Moving was choosing. Each chose. Each followed their own direction. Each direction was parallel. The parallel was the following.

The following was not coordination. The following was each choosing and the choosing arriving at the same place. The same place was not agreement. The same place was direction.

Dael moved. The pull was present. Dael moved before the pull asserted. The movement was Dael's. The ascription was active. The following was each following their own direction and finding the directions parallel.

Day thirty-four ended with the following. The following was enough.
