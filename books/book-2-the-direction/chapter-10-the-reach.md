Day thirty-one. She woke and the pull was there and the pull had an object. Not an object she had chosen. The object had been there. The window had been there. Luyten had been there. The pull had been toward. The toward had been unnamed. This morning the toward had a name. Not a name she spoke. A name the pull knew. The pull knew the window. The pull knew the gold light. The pull knew the route from corridor to junction to window. She walked. The walking reached. Not reached as in arrived. Reached as in extended toward. Each step was a reaching. Each step extended the pull toward the object. The object was the window. The window was where Luyten was visible. The pull was not faster than yesterday. The pull was more specific. Specificity was not speed. Specificity was the pull knowing what it reached toward. Maren was in the corridor. Maren was walking the same direction. Not the same route. The same direction. Maren carried weight too. Not the same weight. Parallel weight. Maren's weight had its own pull. Maren's pull had its own object. They did not speak. The parallel was enough. Walking alongside was enough. The parallel carrying was recognition without naming. Maren did not ask where Dael was going. Dael did not ask where Maren was going. The going was parallel. The parallel was the recognition. She reached the window junction and Recite was there. Recite had been adjacent. Recite was always adjacent. Recite offered window-bearing data. Luyten's position relative to current heading. The offering was practice. Not practice as in repetition. Practice as in Recite's curiosity had habit now. Recite had offered yesterday. Recite offered again today. The offering was not help. The offering was curiosity that had become practice. Curiosity as practice was wanting that offered because offering was what wanting did. Dael accepted. The acceptance was not gratitude. The acceptance was the reaching continuing. She turned toward the window. The window was there. The glass was there. Luyten was there beyond the glass. Gold light through glass. She reached. The reaching was toward the glass. The hand extended. The hand touched the glass. The glass was cool. The glass was flat. The glass was transparent. The glass permitted visibility and blocked touch. Luyten was visible. Luyten was not touchable. The reaching was real. The reaching had arrived at the glass. The reaching had not arrived at Luyten. The not-arriving was not failure. The not-arriving was the glass. The glass was what reaching found when reaching reached far enough. The finding was enough. The hand was on the glass. The glass was there. The reaching was there. The reaching was real. The real was not the object. The real was the reaching. Pollen was at the junction. Pollen saw the hand on the glass. Pollen said yes. The yes was not a question. The yes was not agreement. The yes was resonance. Dael had said yes yesterday. Dael had said yes when Pollen named the carrying. Pollen said yes now. The same word. Different speaker. The word had traveled. The yes had moved from Dael to Pollen. The yes was shared now. The resonance was: I see you reaching and I have reached and the reaching is enough. The hand stayed on the glass. The glass stayed cool. Luyten stayed gold. The reaching stayed. The staying was not holding. The staying was the reaching continuing. The reaching had found its limit. The limit was the glass. The glass was not failure. The glass was what the reaching touched. Touching the glass was reaching. Reaching was pull with a specific object. The object was not graspable. The reaching was real. Day thirty-one ended with her hand on the glass. The glass was frame. She did not know this yet. The frame would come tomorrow. Today the reaching was enough.
